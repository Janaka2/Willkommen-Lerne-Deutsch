So benutzt du diese Seite (How to use this site)

1. Verstehen

Read the Golden Rules and Grammar. Short, clear, with English. Don't memorise tables — understand the logic.

2. Sprechen

Learn the Sentence Patterns by heart. Speak them out loud every day. Language is memory + repetition.

3. Wiederholen

Use Flashcards and Quizzes daily. A little every day beats a lot once a week.
