🎧 Hören & Nachsprechen

Two hands-free audio modes. Listening Loop: lean back and let today's sentences play on repeat — perfect for the commute or chores; lots of input builds your internal model of German. Shadowing: the sentence plays, then you say it out loud, copying the rhythm and melody — this is what turns listening into speaking. Adjust the speed to your level.

Tipp
Listening Loop plays each sentence a few times, then moves on and loops the whole set — keep it running in the background. Shadowing plays once, waits while you repeat aloud, plays once more as a check, then advances. Keep your screen awake so the audio doesn't pause on mobile. „Heute fällig" uses the cards due in your FSRS-Lernkarten.
