Dein Tagesplan 📅

A simple daily routine for intensive learning. Tick each box as you finish. Your progress is saved automatically and resets each new day.

    🔥 So bleibst du dran (How to keep the habit)

    Same time, same place, every day. Even 20 minutes counts. Speak out loud — your mouth needs practice too, not only your eyes. Missed a day? Just start again today.

    Warum dieser Plan? (Why this plan works)

    Aktiv statt passiv

    Reading is not enough. You must speak, write and test yourself. The plan mixes input (grammar, listening) with output (speaking, flashcards).

    Wiederholung (spaced repetition)

    You review a little every day. Words you find hard come back more often in the flashcards. That is how short-term memory becomes long-term memory.
