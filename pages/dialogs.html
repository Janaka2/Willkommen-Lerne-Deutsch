Sprechen mit einer Partnerin / einem Partner

🗣️ Dialoge — echte Gespräche üben

Real conversations for real life — at the phone shop, the doctor, the supermarket, meeting new people, talking about your plans. Read both roles aloud, then turn on Übungsmodus to hide partner B and answer yourself. Click 🔊 to hear any line.

🧠 So übst du (how to practise)
1) Read the whole dialog aloud (both A and B). 2) Switch on Übungsmodus — B's lines blur. 3) Play role A and try to say B's answer from memory; hover/tap a blurred line to check. 4) Swap roles. Do each dialog until it feels easy.
