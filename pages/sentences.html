💬 Satzmuster — flüssig sprechen

These are ready-made sentence frames. Learn the frame by heart, then drop in your own words at ___. Speak each one out loud, many times. This is the fastest way to sound fluent. Click 🔊 to hear it.

🧠 Methode (the method)
Pick 10 patterns. Say each one 10 times aloud. Then build 3 of your own sentences with it. Tomorrow, review them and add 10 new ones. Repetition turns patterns into reflexes.
