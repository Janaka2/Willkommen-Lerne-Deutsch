🧠 Lernkarten (FSRS)

Modern spaced repetition with the FSRS algorithm. Each card comes back right before you'd forget it. Cards are two-way: you recall the meaning (DE → EN) and produce the German (EN → DE). Try to say the answer out loud before you flip — that active recall is what builds speaking fluency. Everything is saved on this device only; use Export to back up or move to another device.

Wie FSRS funktioniert
Be honest with your rating. Nochmal = you forgot (card returns very soon). Schwer = right but hard. Gut = the normal answer. Leicht = effortless (the interval jumps far ahead). FSRS learns your personal forgetting curve and schedules each card for the moment you're about to forget it — aiming for ~90% recall. The classic Leitner Karteikarten deck still works too.
