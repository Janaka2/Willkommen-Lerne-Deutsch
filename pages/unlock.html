🔓 Sprechen freischalten

Learn one sentence by heart. Then change only one thing at a time — the time, the person, the form. You'll produce dozens of correct sentences from a single frame and feel the conjugation, no tables required. Always: read the English, say the German out loud first 🗣️, then reveal and listen 🔊.

🧭 So funktioniert's
Pick a seed sentence → pick one track → say each line aloud from the English cue → reveal to check (only the changed word lights up) → at the end, say the whole row in one breath.
