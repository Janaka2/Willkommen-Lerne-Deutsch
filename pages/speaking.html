🎤 Sprechen & Sätze bauen

Listening builds the model in your head — but producing German is what makes you speak. Two modes: Sprich die Antwort uses your microphone (Web Speech API) to hear you say the German and check it. Satz bauen makes you build a whole sentence yourself from an English prompt, then reveals the model answer with case colours so you see exactly which grammar made it right. Speech recognition works best in Chrome/Edge.

Modus:
⚠️ Spracherkennung nicht verfügbar
Dein Browser unterstützt die Spracherkennung (SpeechRecognition) nicht — am besten funktioniert sie in Chrome oder Edge. Du kannst trotzdem üben: sprich laut für dich und nutze „Antwort zeigen" zum Vergleichen. Im Modus „Satz bauen" kannst du deine Antwort auch tippen.

Try to produce the sentence before you reveal — a wrong guess you then correct sticks far better than a right answer you only read. The first time you use the mic, your browser will ask for microphone permission. The model answers reuse the same Nom · Akk · Dat · Gen colours as the rest of the site.
